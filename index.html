About Us

Welcome to Club Katz Café, where coffee meets cats in the coziest ambiance in town.
Our café is not just a place to grab a drink;
it's a sanctuary where cat lovers can unwind, work, or simply enjoy the company of our resident felines. Experience the joy of purring companions with your favorite brew in hand.

Our Menu

Our menu features a variety of handcrafted coffees, teas, and pastries, all made with the finest ingredients. From our signature Katzpuccino to freshly baked cat-themed cookies and cakes, there’s something for everyone. Don’t forget to try our special Catnip Tea, a hit among our feline friends.

Cat Happy Hour

Join us for our exclusive Cat Happy Hour – a rave for cats and their human companions. These special events feature dimmed lights, soft electronic music, and plenty of interactive toys for our cats to enjoy. It’s the purrfect way to see the café come alive with energy and excitement.
